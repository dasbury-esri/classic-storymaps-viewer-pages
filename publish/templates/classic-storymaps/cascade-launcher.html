What is an app ID?

It is a 32-character hexadecimal ID used to identify Cascade stories in ArcGIS Online.
Example: 4e076f0a2ca141bab12ad7f1bbdf8571

  • They use letters a-f and numbers 0-9 without spaces, braces or hyphens.
  • Invalid IDs will be checked before launch.
